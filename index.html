FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How fast can a technician arrive for emergency plumbing? +
Our average response time for local emergency calls is under 60 minutes. We have dispatchers on call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to handle leaks, pipe breaks, and sewer backups.
Are your plumbers licensed and insured? +
Yes, 100% of our service technicians are fully licensed, background-checked, and comprehensive liability insured to provide premium service and peace of mind.
Do you provide flat-rate pricing or hourly estimates? +
We provide transparent, flat-rate pricing. After analyzing the issue, our technician will provide an upfront written estimate detailing all costs. You won't face any surprise fees or hourly rate overruns.
Do you offer a warranty on repairs and parts? +
Yes, all parts we supply and our labor are backed by our service guarantee, ranging from 1 to 5 years depending on the scope of the plumbing work performed.
