Our History

Fixing leaks & restoring comfort every day since 2015

For over a decade, MasterCare has provided local homeowners with premium, dependable plumbing and home repair services. We started with a single truck and a commitment to transparent service. Today, we are proud to serve thousands of happy customers with a full fleet of response units.

Our core philosophy is simple: diagnose the issue correctly, explain the solution clearly, give an upfront price before any work begins, and back every single repair with an ironclad service warranty.

Whether you have a minor faucet drip or require emergency main sewer pipe excavations, our team combines the expert technical diagnostics, high-performance equipment, and respectful customer service you deserve.

Fully Licensed & Insured

All of our field technicians are background-checked, certified, and fully insured. We operate strictly in compliance with all local codes.

Upfront Written Quotes

No mystery fees, no hourly tricks. You will get a detailed flat-rate price quote detailing all work before our technicians touch a tool.

Durable Service Warranties

We stand behind the quality of our repairs. All parts and labor are guaranteed with warranty protection to give you ultimate peace of mind.
